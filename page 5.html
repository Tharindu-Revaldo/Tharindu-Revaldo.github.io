About Broke House Pictures

Our Story

Broke House Pictures is an independent film production house founded by passionate filmmaker Tharindu Revaldo. Our journey began with a vision: to tell authentic, bold stories that resonate with audiences—no matter the budget.

We specialize in feature films, documentaries, and short films. From concept to final cut, we handle every step with creativity, technical skill, and a relentless drive for excellence.

Whether it’s a raw, personal documentary or a cinematic short film, we believe in the power of storytelling to inspire, challenge, and connect people. Our team thrives on collaboration and innovation, bringing each project to life with unique vision and heart.
